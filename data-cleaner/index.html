Career Center Data Cleaner

December 2014

Project Type

University Project

Software Used

Choregraphe

Languages Used

Python

Primary Role(s)

Lead Programmer, Graphic Designer

Description

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Iowa designed a new interdisciplinary, project-based course in collaboration with the Department of Dance in which students designed and implemented a 45-minute dance recital performed by autonomous humanoid robots.

I created a dance routine for this performance, and continued to work with the robots through the University to create several theatrical skits, as well as to do outreach to encourage children to continue STEM education.
